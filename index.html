Fractions Fundamentals

What are Fractions?

A fraction represents a part of a whole. Imagine cutting a pizza into equal slices. If you have 3 slices out of 8 total slices, you have 3/8 of the pizza.

Think About It

The top number (numerator) tells you how many parts you have. The bottom number (denominator) tells you how many equal parts the whole is divided into.

Visual Representation

3/5 — 3 parts out of 5 are highlighted
